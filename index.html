Think
Read the prompt. Think independently. Build your own answer before discussing it.

What pupils should do

  • Think: work silently and decide on your own best response.
  • Pair: compare ideas, challenge each other, and improve your answer.
  • Share: be ready to explain clearly, justify your thinking, and speak to the group.

Why it works

  • It gives every pupil thinking time before anyone speaks.
  • It increases participation, not just from the most confident pupils.
  • It improves the quality of answers before whole-class discussion.
Teacher tip: circulate during Pair, listen for strong answers, then use Share to lift the best thinking into the whole class.
